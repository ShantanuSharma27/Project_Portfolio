Services

As a web developer, I specialize in building custom websites for small to medium-sized businesses. Here are some of the services I offer:

  • Website design and development: I can create a modern, responsive website that accurately reflects your brand and meets the needs of your target audience.
  • E-commerce development: If you're looking to sell products online, I can build you a fully-functional e-commerce website that integrates with your payment and shipping providers.
  • Website maintenance and support: Once your website is live, I can provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure it stays up-to-date and secure.
  • Website optimization: I can help optimize your website for search engines, making it easier for potential customers to find you online.
